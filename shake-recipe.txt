Equipment:
 - A powerful blender such as Vitamix

Ingredients:
 - 16 oz water
 - 2 tbsp chia seeds or 4 tbsp raw flax seeds
 - 2 scoops protein powder
 - 12 oz frozen strawberries or blueberries

Directions:
 1. Pour water into blender
 2. Add seeds to blender
 3. Add powder to blender
 4. Add fruit to blender
 5. Run blender until contents are liquified
 
Makes 2 servings
